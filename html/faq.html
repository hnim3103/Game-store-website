How will I receive the game after purchase?

Immediately after your payment is successfully processed, you will receive a digital activation key (also known as a product key or CD key) for your game.

This key will be sent to the email address you used to register your account. You can also view your key at any time in the 'Order History' or 'My Games Library' section of your account on our website.

Can I get a refund if I no longer want to play the game?
